TimeSearcher

    TimeSearcher was created by Harry Hochheiser in Java as TimeSearcher 1.
    Parts of  TimeSearcher 1 was rewritten in C# by Amir Khella as TimeSearcher 2.
The pattern search was added in cooperation with Paolo Buono.
    Aleks Aris is the current person working on TimeSearcher 2.

    The TimeSearcher project is being developed under the supervision of
Catherine Plaisant and Ben Shneiderman.
